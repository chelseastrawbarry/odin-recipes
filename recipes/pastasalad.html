Pasta Salad

This pasta salad combines fresh ingredients with yummy sauces and spices that everyone will enjoy!

Cucumbers, celery, bell peppers and red onions are paired with mayonaise, dill pickle relish and a hint of italian dressing to bring the dish to life.

I prefer to use rotini pasta but bowtie or penne works well for this dish too!

Ingredients

  • 1 box Rotini Pasta
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper (diced)
  • 1 Cucumber (diced)
  • 1 Small Red Onion (diced)
  • 3 Celery Stalks (diced)
  • 1 Block of Sharp Cheddar Cheese (diced)
  • 1/2 Package of Pepperoni (Diced)
  • 1 cup of Mayonaise
  • 1/2 Cup Dill Pickle Relish
  • 1/4 Cup Italian Dressing
  • 1/4 Cup of McCormick's Pasta Salad Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente, rinse with cold water and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, dice cucumber, bell pepper, celery, onion, cheese and pepperoni and set aside.
  3. Combine pasta with chopped ingredients into a large bowl.
  4. Add 1 cup mayonaise, 1/2 cup dill pickle relish, 1/4 cup of italian dressing and 1/4 cup of McCormick's Pasta Salad Seasoning to bowl.
  5. Stir until ingredients are well combined.
  6. Let cool in the fridge for 1 hour before serving.
